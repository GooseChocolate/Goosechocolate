Wearing Sunglasses For Too Long May Cause Blindness

It's a common thing to see sunglasses worn by blind people, but not many know the real reason. Based on a study conducted by four scientists at Goose Chocolate, wearing sunglasses for an extended period of time may lead to blindness. This is because of the polycarbonate lenses lessening the intensity of light, tricking your eyes into not needing to work as hard. This eye relaxation results in vision loss and, if sunglasses are worn too often, even blindness. The four bright scientists who conducted this research advise everyone to only wear sunglasses when necessary. Due to the majority of the population having very similar structured eyes, everyone is at risk.
