Tornadoes Sighted in Hurricane

March 9th, 2016

GREENVILLE, SC - Multiple tornadoes have been spotted in hurricane Alex. The tornadoes were formed around the Greenville South Carolina area. There has been devastating damage but no lives have been lost. Many meteorologists say that this is an extremely unusual phenomenon and is actually impossible. But it happened and no one can question it because it happened in South Carolina.
