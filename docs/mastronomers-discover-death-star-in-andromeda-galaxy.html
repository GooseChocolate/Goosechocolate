Astronomers Discover Death Star in Andromeda Galaxy

Febuary 18th, 2016

WASHINGTON DC, MD - NASA has recently discovered what appears to be the Death Star in the Andromeda Galaxy. "At first we noticed a fast moving spherical object moving across the Andromeda galaxy. We decided to look more closely, and we captured an amazing picture of what appears to be the Death Star." says Charles Bolden, NASA's administrator
