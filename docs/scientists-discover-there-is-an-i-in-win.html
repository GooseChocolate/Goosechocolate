Scientists Discover There is an "I" in "Win"

Febuary 12th, 2016

CAMBRIDGE, MA - After years of pondering and lab experimentation, scientists at the prestigious technical school Harvard University have discovered there is in fact an "I" in "win".

We have heard for years that there is no 'I' in 'team', but little did those thousands of coaches around the world know, there is an 'I' in 'win'," says George Lucas, a professor at Harvard University. "We have been decieved our entire lives," says an anonymous football player at Penn State. Hopefully, college students all throughout the United States can take the important advice to always think about yourself during all kinds of sports.
