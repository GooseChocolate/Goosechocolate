Trump Called Out by Pope Francis

Febuary 24th, 2016

PAPAL AIRLINER - Donald J. Trump was recently said to not be a Christian by Pope Francis. "A person who thinks only about building walls, wherever they may be located, and not about building bridges, is not a Christian." says the Pope.

Trump, after hearing this news and knowing that being a Christian is the most important aspect of becoming the president of a country where freedom of religion is 100% allowed, fired back at Pope Francis by saying, "For a religious leader to question a person's faith is disgraceful."
