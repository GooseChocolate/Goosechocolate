Apple Unveils New iRobot Prototype

Febuary 4th, 2016

CUPERTINO, CA - Saturday, Febuary 3, Apple unveiled the new iRobot prototype. According to Apple, the iRobot will have brand new features such as cleaning dishes, cleaning rooms, walking the dog, getting the mail, being controlled by an evil self aware artificial intelligence named VIKI, taking over a majority of the first world, hanging up pictures, etc. Apple plans to release the new iRobot Febuary 30th, 2016 if all goes as planned.
