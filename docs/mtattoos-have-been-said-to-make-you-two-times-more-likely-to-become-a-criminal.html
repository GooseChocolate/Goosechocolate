Tattoos Have Been Said to Make You Two Times More Likely to Become a Criminal

Febuary 25th, 2016

Based on a Goose Chocolate survey, people with tattoos are, on average, almost two times as likely to make you do something illegal. It seems like a number of factors are taken into consideration when it comes to the tattoo and illegal actions correlation that include how large the tattoo is, the amount of color used in the tattoo, and the overall symbolism that it has. These factors also are used when relating to the severity of the crime committed.
