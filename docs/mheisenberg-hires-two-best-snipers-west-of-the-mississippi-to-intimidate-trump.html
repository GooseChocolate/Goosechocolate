Heisenberg Hires Two Best Snipers West of the Mississippi to Intimidate Trump

Febuary 5th, 2016

NEW YORK CITY, NY - Heisenberg, better known as Walter White, hired the "two best snipers west of the Mississippi" to intimidate Donald Trump during his speaches. A picture was captured that shows two small red dots on Trump's torso, showing that the two assassins were ready to shoot. Trump has said that he has improved his security to keep him safe, although he still must comply with the assassin's vauge wishes. The same two assassins have been noted to also have followed around Gretchen and Elliot Schwartz.
