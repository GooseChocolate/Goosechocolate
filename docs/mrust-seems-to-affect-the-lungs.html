Rust Seems to Affect the Lungs

Febuary 10th, 2016

CAMBRIDGE, MA - Rust. The product of oxygen touching iron, or is it just iron? A group of students at MIT has discovered that oxygen has the same effect on our lungs that it does on iron. "Wake up, America!" says a concerned mother about the safety of her children's lungs because of the terrible chemical. "We have discovered that the rusting of the lungs takes approximately 80 years to kill humans, which is why the life span has been limited all of these years. All we need to do to increase our life span is to stop breathing!" says one of the students.
