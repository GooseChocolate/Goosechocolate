Teens Fined for Double Dipping

Febuary 9th, 2016

ROCHESTER, NY - "I just never thought I would see the day," says concerned mother of one of the two teens who were fined earlier this week for double dipping. At a local birthday party, two teens were caught taking a bit of a chip, then proceeding to put the chip back into the bowl. "We're just so disguisted! We had to shut down the party because of these two misbehaven children!" says the host of the party. "Reports of double dipping have been increasing at an alarming rate. Double dipping crimes have almost tripled since 2009. Awareness of double dipping must be spread and the germs must be stopped for good!" says the local sheriff.
