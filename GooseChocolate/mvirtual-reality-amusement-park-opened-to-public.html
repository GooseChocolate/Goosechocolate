Virtual Reality Amusement Park Opened to Public

Febuary 29th, 2016

SALT LAKE CITY, UT - A virtual reality amusement park was recently opened to the public and it seems to be extremely popular among gamers across the United States. The same company that developed and built this amusement park also plan on creating many other virtual reality experiences such as grocery shopping, walking the dog, and even going outside. Technology is advancing so much more quickly than anyone could have ever imagined. Maybe someday we could have virtual reality social lives.
