Two Sheetz Have Been Built Within 10 Feet Of Each Other

Febuary 16th, 2016

RENO, NV - Two Sheetz gas station and convinience stores were placed within 10 feet of eachother in an empty parking lot in Reno, Nevada. "We don't even know if its legal, but who cares?! We're Sheetz!" says Joseph Sheetz, CEO of Sheetz. "No more waiting in line for gas so who cares!" says Hillary Schieve, mayor of Reno and a fan of Sheetz since she was 12. Most citizens love the idea of having two Sheetz side by side, but there are some outside of Sheetz peacefully protesting.
