A Vote Recount Leads to Leonardo Decaprio Winning an Oscar

Febuary 17th, 2016

LOS ANGELES, CA - Because of the number of protests of unfair judging at the Oscars, judges have revoted and Leonardo Decaprio has finally won an Oscar. Many believed that Decaprio has deserved an Oscar for years and now that he has finally won one, the countless unfunny internet memes can finally stop.
