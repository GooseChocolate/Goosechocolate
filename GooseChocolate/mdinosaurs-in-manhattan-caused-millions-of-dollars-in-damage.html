Dinosaurs in Manhattan Caused Millions of Dollars in Damage

Febuary 19th, 2016

NEW YORK CITY, NY - Dinosaurs continue to rule the streets of Manhattan beginning Febuary 13th. So far, they have caused millions of dollars in damage and dinosaurs such as the stegosauraus, triceratops, and infamous t-rex have been seen ripping cars in half and smashing buildings to the ground. The national guard has been alerted and have been trying to fight off the dinosaurs. So far there have been as many as 13,000 casualties. The dinosaurs have not been letting up at all.
