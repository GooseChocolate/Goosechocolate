Deadpool Twitter Account Claims to have Hacked Twitter

Febuary 8th, 2016

SAN FRANCISCO, CA - The Deadpool Twitter accound claimed to have hacked Twitter and made a tweet longer than the usual 140 characters.

This, of course, could potentially ruin Deadpool's career, as this is a very serious matter. This could possibly ruin Twitter forever if directions on how to complete this hack leaks out, as accounts could make infinitely long tweets. Twitter must fix this bug, because if Deadpool can do it, anyone can.
